About Us

Discover the story behind King's Chips and our commitment to serving the best seafood in Sydney.

Our Story

Founded in 1995, King's Chips began as a small fish and chips shop in Sydney. With a passion for quality seafood and traditional cooking methods, we've grown to become one of the city's most beloved seafood restaurants.

Our Philosophy

Fresh Ingredients

We source our seafood daily from local fishermen to ensure the highest quality and freshness in every dish.

Traditional Recipes

Our cooking methods have been passed down through generations, preserving the authentic taste of classic fish and chips.

Community Focus

We're proud to be part of the Sydney community and actively support local events and initiatives.
